01About

Chartered-level Civil Engineer with 25+ years' experience delivering safety-critical surface transport and highway engineering schemes across London, including extensive work on the TfL Road Network (TLRN) and TfL-funded borough projects.

Proven capability across the full project lifecycle — feasibility, design, stakeholder consultation, statutory processes, construction and handover — with a strong focus on network safety, asset performance, active travel, lighting and junction improvements. Experienced in managing consultants and contractors under TfL and local authority governance, CDM 2015 and NEC frameworks. Currently undertaking postgraduate research at University College London (UCL) in pedestrian–cyclist interaction and non-motorised transport, strengthening evidence-led and safety-focused engineering decision-making.
