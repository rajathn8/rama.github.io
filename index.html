01About

Chartered-level Civil Engineer with 25+ years' experience delivering safety-critical surface transport and highway engineering schemes across London, including extensive work on the TfL Road Network (TLRN) and TfL-funded borough projects.

Proven capability across the full project lifecycle — feasibility, design, stakeholder consultation, statutory processes, construction and handover — with a strong focus on network safety, asset performance, active travel, lighting and junction improvements. 04Applied research

Active research at University College London (UCL), conducted alongside professional delivery work — sharpening the evidence base behind safer, inclusive street design.

Pedestrian & cyclist conflict in shared, non-motorised environments

Combining qualitative and quantitative observation methods to characterise conflict points in shared and non-motorised spaces — with practical implications for junction design, crossing geometry, signage, and active-travel infrastructure.

  • Active Travel
  • Healthy Streets
  • Inclusive design
  • Behavioural observation
  • Safer urban realm

Why it matters for the work I deliver

Findings feed directly into day-to-day scheme design — helping me question assumptions in junction layouts, school streets, cycle routes, and shared-surface treatments, and back up design decisions with documented evidence during stakeholder consultation and road safety audit.
